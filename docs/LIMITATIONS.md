# Limitations

- **Synthetic data** — prices are realistic for learning, not official CPI or market quotes.
- **Seven categories only** — does not cover every household expense (rent, medicine, school fees, etc.).
- **Monthly granularity** — short spikes within a month are smoothed out.
- **No forecasting** — charts describe the past; they do not predict future tariffs or inflation.

For official statistics, see the [Department of Census and Statistics, Sri Lanka](https://www.statistics.gov.lk/).
